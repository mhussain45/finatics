Funding The Future: The Ecolving Landscape of Startup Finance

Date October 15, 2025
Time 3:15 PM
Venue F2 Seminar hall, F Block, Amity University Noida

Students gained direct access to the minds of successful entrepreneurs and experts during a session focused on the "new rules" of startup funding. From crowdfunding to angel investing, the panel broke down the shifting financial dynamics of the startup world and provided a roadmap for the next generation to sharpen their skills and seize emerging opportunities.

Finatics, the finance club of Amity School of Business, brought the world of venture capital and entrepreneurship to F Block this October. The panel, "Funding the Future," featured a diverse lineup of founders and specialists who shared firsthand accounts of navigating the startup ecosystem. Students walked away with a clearer understanding of how to secure backing and manage growth, thanks to the collective expertise of Dr. Anubhav Gupta, Mr. Dhruv Arora, and Mr. Kanchann Singh Kuwarbi, who highlighted everything from branding strategies to the challenges of modern fundraising.
