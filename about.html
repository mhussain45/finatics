About Finatics

Where Finance Meets Strategy

Who We Are

Finatics is a student-run finance club under faculty supervision, focused on building financial literacy, strategic thinking, and real-world market exposure.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic finance and real-world application by encouraging analytical thinking, collaboration, and informed decision-making.

Our Vision

To develop future finance professionals and strategic thinkers through practical learning, events, and community engagement.

What We Do

We organize finance workshops, speaker sessions, competitions, market discussions, and learning initiatives across all finance domains.
